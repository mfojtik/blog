Michal Fojtik Bio

Michal is a Principal Software Engineer at Red Hat. For the past six years, he’s been actively contributing to various open-source projects. Currently, Michal works full-time on the OpenShift project, a PaaS based on Google Kubernetes, where he is a member of the developer experience team. His involvement with OpenShift includes designing continuous integration solutions based on OpenShift and Kubernetes and co-maintaining the OpenShift image building subsystem.
